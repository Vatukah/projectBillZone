Why Choose Us ?

BillZone can be configured for almost any time entry environment. It allows you to continue projects as they change over time, including as customer contracts and billing rates change. This means that you always have the bigger, complete picture at hand, instead of creating a new project or invoice whenever your rates or terms change (as you see in other time systems). BillZone is great for those businesses who are more than just a single estimate and invoice. BillZone is for businesses that have long term relationships with their customers. It is a system designed for your situation by those who work like you do!
